E-HORYZON’24 : Igniting Innovation and Entrepreneurship


Welcome to E-HORYZON’24, an exciting initiative by the Innovation & Entrepreneurship Forum (IEF) at Kongu Engineering College. Our mission is to inspire innovation, empower students, and foster an entrepreneurial spirit.


What Is E-HORYZON’24?

  • E-HORYZON’24 is an intra-college event organized by the Engineering, Manufacturing, and Design Club (EMDC) in collaboration with the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC).
  • It’s a dynamic platform where students can showcase their talents, creativity, and problem-solving abilities.

Key Objectives :

  1. Entrepreneurship Awareness : Ignite your entrepreneurial spark! Explore real-world challenges and discover your passion for impactful solutions.
  2. Creating an Entrepreneurial Culture : E-HORYZON’24 encourages risk-taking, creativity, and resilience.
  3. Skill Development and Opportunities : Gain practical skills, network with experts, and explore diverse career paths.
  4. Turning Ideas into Prototypes : Transform abstract concepts into tangible solutions.

Get Involved!

  • Participate : Engage in hackathons, workshops, and competitions.
  • Collaborate : Connect with peers and mentors.
  • Learn : Attend talks and skill-building sessions.
  • Celebrate Innovations : Join us on this exciting journey!
