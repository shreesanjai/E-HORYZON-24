Event Rules


  1. A team can consist maximum of 2 members.

  2. Registration fee Rs.200/team.

  3. Participants will be given the circuit to be debugged and instructed to develop a PCB layout with the help of simulation software (Proteus).

  4. With the help of the PCB file generated, participants will be asked to develop a PCB working model (At TBI).

  5. The organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules of the contest at any point of time and will do their best to inform participants of the same through WhatsApp group.
